# Lists and Pseudocode

In this topic, you’ll expand your Python toolkit with a new data type: **Lists**.

Sometimes you want to store a lot of related pieces of information in one place. Maybe you’re writing a music app and need to save all of the songs on a playlist. Or tracking all of the past scores for a baseball team to predict their wins next season. Lists are here to help!

Now that your programs are becoming more complex, we’ll also talk about another useful tool that you’ll see used by programmers in every industry: **Writing Pseudocode**.
